About us

Welcome to STICKSTATS — a modern hockey platform delivering live match updates, tournament coverage, and player highlights for school and community-level hockey.

Built for speed and simplicity, STICKSTATS brings real-time scores, verified match data, and curated highlights directly to fans, players, and organizers.

We keep your profile visible, your performance recognized, and your achievements highlighted — both on and off the field.

Our Mission

To empower grassroots and school-level hockey by providing accurate live scoring, player recognition, and a digital presence for every match and athlete.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted digital ecosystem for community hockey, connecting players, fans, and organizers through technology.

What We Offer

Live Match Updates

Real-time scores, goals, cards, and match events updated instantly by verified admins.

Player Profiles

Digital profiles showcasing player stats, achievements, and match highlights.

Tournament Management

Seamless tournament listings, fixtures, standings, and match history.
