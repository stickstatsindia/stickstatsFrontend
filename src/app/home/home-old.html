About Our Platform

At StickStats, we are redefining the way hockey is experienced. Our platform allows players, teams, and fans to track live scores, analyze detailed statistics, and celebrate every moment of the game.

From grassroots matches to competitive leagues, StickStats captures every performance, helping players improve and giving fans a place to stay connected with the sport they love.

We believe in empowering the hockey community with technology, making the game more exciting, engaging, and data-driven than ever before.
